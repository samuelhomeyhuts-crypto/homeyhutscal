Payout Calculation Guide

Airbnb

Guest pays total → Airbnb and We deducts 10% Homeyhuts service fee → Remaining amount is released to host (usually within 24 hours after check‑in).

MakeMyTrip

Guest pays total → MakeMyTrip and We deducts 10% Homeyhuts service fee. Payout = Final Price - 15% commission.

Homeyhuts

Guest pays final price → GST applied (5% if base ≤ ₹7,500, else 18%) → Base Price calculated → 14% service fee deducted → Remaining amount is host payout.

Agoda

Guest pays final price → GST applied (5% if ≤ ₹7,500, else 18%) → Base Price calculated → 10% service fee deducted → Remaining amount is host payout.
